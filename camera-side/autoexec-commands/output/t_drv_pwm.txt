Usage: pwm [start|stop|status|unitest] <...>
       pwm start <channel> <freq> <OnTicks> <OffTicks> : start pwm <channel> in <freq>Hz
       pwm stop <channel>                              : stop pwm <channel> 
       pwm status <channel>                            : get pwm status of <channel>
       channel_num's range is 0~6, 5 for 1/2 Complementary, 6 for 3/4 Complementary
       PWM rate = freq / (OnTicks + OffTicks)
